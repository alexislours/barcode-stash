# What's New

## 1.1.0 (2026022301) - 2026-02-23

- Scan barcodes from photos in your library or via the Share Sheet from any app
- Home Screen quick actions: long-press the app icon to jump straight to Scan, Create, or Favorites
- Preview and save contacts from vCard QR codes, and add calendar events from vEvent codes
- Compose SMS messages directly from scanned QR codes with multi-recipient support
- Redesigned barcode action cards with color-coded icons and detailed payload breakdowns
- Redesigned selection mode for batch operations
- Improved search: accented characters now match unaccented queries (e.g. "cafe" finds "cafe")
- Camera permission denied screen now shows a clear message with a link to Settings
- Numerous performance improvements: faster scrolling, reduced memory usage, smoother sharing
- Improved reliability for barcode deletion, bulk tagging, and Data Matrix rendering
- Better VoiceOver support across the scanner, filter menu, and tag editor
- Various stability fixes and crash prevention improvements

## 1.0.0 (2026021701) - 2026-02-17

Barcode Stash is here! Scan, create, and organize your barcodes in one place.

- Scan barcodes instantly with your camera, including bulk scanning mode
- Create barcodes in 11 formats with live preview and validation
- Advanced options for QR, Aztec, and PDF417 barcodes
- Organize with favorites, tags, notes, and search
- View where you scanned each barcode on a map
- Sync across all your devices with iCloud
- Export and import your barcode collection as JSON or CSV
- Smart QR actions for links, contacts, WiFi, calendar events, and more
- Available in 10 languages
- No tracking, no ads, no data collection - your barcodes stay yours
